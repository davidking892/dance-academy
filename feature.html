CONTEMPORARY DANCE

Contemporary Dance is a style of expressive dance form. It is a genre of dance performance that improves physical control, balance, coordination, alignment, strength, flexibility and stamina. Reflecting the situation in society at large, contemporary dance is progressively integrating overtly technological elements and in particular Robotic.

SALSA

Being a beautiful yet challenging form of dance, Salsa requires blend of physical synchronization and metal harmony between two partners so as to achieve the desired results of good chemistry and great achievements. Salsa generally uses music ranging from about 150 bpm ( beats per minute) to around 250 bpm, it may vary from 160- 220 bpm.

HIP HOP

Hip Hop Dance represents the freedom to learn, grow and evolve. Hip- Hop Culture is the expression of four elements: Breaking, Rapping, Graphite and Djing. What distinguishes Hip- Hop from other dance forms is that it is often “freestyle” in nature and it crews often employ in freestyle dance competitions- vernacularly referred to as “battles”. Hip Hop dance can be a form of enjoyment or an avocation.

ZUMBA

Zumba is a Latin inspired dance workout. Its all about grooving to the beats of Salsa, flamenco, and merengue music feels more like a dance party than a workout. “Ditch the Workout, and Join the Party” is our tag line for Zumba Classes. Zumba is an interval workout and helps to burn on an average 369 calories- more than cardio kicking boxing or step aerobics.

BOLLYWOOD

It is a dance form that has spread itself equally among an ever growing population of above a billion people separated by the diversities of region, religion, castes, languages and many other cultural differences. Currently it is influenced by Western Culture, its evolution is a process that is entirely Indian and yet cross- cultural at the same time.

BREAK DANCE

Break Dance also known as B- Boying/ B- Girling or BreakDancing is an athletic style of street dance. It is an energetic form of dance that includes stylized footwork and athletic moves such as spinning on the knees, hands or head. It is a dynamic style of dance and one of the major elements of Hip- Hop culture.

ROBOTICS

Robotics Dance Style as the name suggests dancing like a Robot. In this dance form, the dancer moves their limbs in a way that imitates the movements of a Robot. It is also considered a subsection of popping. Movements of the Robot are normally started and finished with dimestop to give the impression of motors starting and stopping.

LYRICAL

Lyrical Dance is a fusion of Ballet and Jazz Dance. It is oriented towards the dancer’s emotional responses rather than to an underlying formal choreographic structure. This dance style concentrates on an individual approach and expressiveness of emotions of love, joy and anger.

CLASSICAL

Indian Classical Dance or Shastriya Nritya is a term for various perfrmance arts rooted I religious Hindu Culture.Unlike folk dances, Classical Dance is all about technicalities and strict rules. In Classical dance ‘mudras’ or signs of hands are used for common language of expression. World famous Indian Classical forms that are originated and evolved in India are Bharatnatyam, Kathak, Kathakali, Kuchipudi, Manipuri, Mohiniattam and Odissi.
