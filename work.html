ABOUT US

DANCING STAR ACADEMY was founded on 13th of feb 2019 by Rakhi Gupta with a belief that every person can grow and gain the skills as a dancer.We constantly endeavour to provide a premier dance education worthy of our eminent community status.

WHAT IS OUR GOAL?

DANCING STAR ACADEMY aims to provide quality dance training and making dance available to all. DSA is a well- known dance academy in faridabad, we offer dance classes to every age group person in a family- friendly, safe and supportive environment.
