FEEL HIGH, FEEL DANCE

Western Dance also known as Country Dance, circumscribes many dance form or styles, which are typically danced to country- western music. Many distinct dances are done to country-western music. These dances include: Two Step, Waltz, Cowboy or Traveling Cha Cha,Polka Ten Step, Schottische, and other Western promenade dances, East Coast Swing, West Coast Swing, and Nightclub Two Step.
